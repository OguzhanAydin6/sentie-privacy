# AuraMi — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **AuraMi**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and delete information when you use the AuraMi mobile application.

AuraMi is designed for personal discovery, entertainment, and positive interaction. It does not provide medical, psychological, therapeutic, diagnostic, or professional advice.

---

## 2. Photos & Camera Use

AuraMi uses your device’s camera and photo library only to enable the following app experiences:

- **Aura Analysis (Auranı Keşfet)**
- **Soul Connection**

### Important:

- Photos are **processed only for the active in-app experience**
- Photos are **not stored permanently**
- Photos are **not uploaded to our servers**
- Photos are **not used for advertising**
- Photos are **not used for user tracking**

Any image you capture or select is used only to generate the on-screen aura-themed result requested by you and is discarded when the session ends.

---

## 3. Face Data and Camera-Based Analysis

AuraMi may use your device camera or a selected image to provide camera-based aura-themed visual experiences. These features are designed only for entertainment and personal discovery purposes.

AuraMi does **not** use face data for facial recognition, identity verification, biometric identification, authentication, user tracking, advertising profiling, or any real-world identification purpose.

Face data is **not retained**. Photos, camera frames, or face-related visual information are used only during the active analysis session and are discarded when the session ends.

AuraMi does **not** permanently store face data on the device or on our servers. AuraMi does **not** upload face data to our servers.

Face data is **not shared with third parties**. AuraMi does not sell, rent, disclose, transfer, or provide face data to advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, service providers, or any other third parties.

Because face data is not retained or shared, third parties do **not** store face data on behalf of AuraMi.

The only reason AuraMi temporarily processes camera or image data is to generate the on-screen aura-themed visual result requested by the user. This temporary processing lasts only for the duration of the active analysis session.

---

## 4. Scan History & Local Storage

When you choose to save an analysis:

- Only **non-image data** is stored, such as aura colors, scores, symbolic results, and analysis text
- This data is saved **locally on your device**
- Camera images, photos, face data, or camera frames are **not saved** as part of scan history
- You may delete this data at any time from within the app

AuraMi does not use saved scan history for advertising, tracking, or biometric identification.

---

## 5. User-Generated Text

Some features allow you to enter personal text, such as reflective prompts or expression-based inputs.

This text is used only to generate on-screen guidance or in-app responses during your session. It is **not saved**, **not retained**, and **not transmitted to our servers** unless a feature clearly states otherwise.

The “Kalbine Ağırlık Yapan Neyse” feature is designed as a temporary expression space. Text entered in this area is not stored after the session ends.

---

## 6. Account and Limited Social Interaction Data

As part of optional account features and the in-app friend system, AuraMi processes a limited set of account-related data on the server side.

This data may include:

- User-defined display name
- Date of birth
- Selected core energies
- System-assigned AuraMi Identification Number (AuraMi ID)
- Friend connection records
- Last activity timestamps
- Basic interaction records needed for app functionality

Light messages within the application are sent using predefined message options. No free-text message content is stored for these light messages.

However, in order for the system to function properly, limited interaction records may be stored, such as:

- Which predefined message was sent
- Sender and recipient identifiers
- Time of delivery
- Delivery status

These data are used only for:

- Managing friend requests
- Delivering light messages
- Enforcing daily usage limits
- Operating the point and reward systems
- Maintaining basic app functionality and safety

Users may request deletion of their account data and associated social interaction records at any time through the in-app **Delete Account** option. Once the account deletion process is completed, the relevant data stored on the server are removed from the system.

---

## 7. Sharing

If you choose to share a result:

- Only the **text, image, or content you explicitly choose** is shared
- AuraMi does not automatically share personal data
- AuraMi does not automatically post content on your behalf
- AuraMi does not share camera images, face data, or analysis results with third parties unless you manually choose to share content outside the app

You are responsible for any content you choose to share outside AuraMi.

---

## 8. Advertising

AuraMi may display optional advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

Advertisements are shown only when the user chooses to view them, such as optional rewarded ads.

These advertising services may automatically collect limited information, which may include:

- Device type and operating system
- IP address for general location estimation
- Advertising identifiers, such as IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android
- Basic interaction data related to ad performance

AuraMi does **not** access, store, or use camera images, face data, aura analysis images, personal reflective text, or account data for advertising purposes.

AuraMi does **not** sell personal data.

Users can control advertising and tracking permissions through their device settings. On iOS, users may also manage tracking permissions through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency settings.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

AuraMi is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

---

## 10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access and delete locally stored data
- Delete your account from within the app
- Control camera, photo library, notification, advertising, and tracking permissions through your device settings
- Contact us regarding privacy concerns
- Request deletion of server-side account and social interaction data associated with your AuraMi account

---

## 11. Account Deletion

Users can delete their account at any time directly from within the app:

**Settings → Delete Account**

When an account is deleted:

- Associated personal account data is permanently removed from our servers
- The AuraMi ID is deleted
- Friend connection records linked to the account are erased
- Social interaction records linked to the account are removed
- This action cannot be undone

No personal account data is retained after account deletion, except where retention is required by law or necessary for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
